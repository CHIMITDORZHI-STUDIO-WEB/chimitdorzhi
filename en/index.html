Q&A

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to build a website, a bot or implement AI?

It depends on the task and scope. After a short conversation I give an honest estimate and offer options that fit your budget — without pushing anything extra.

Do you work remotely or only in Chita and Zabaikalye?

I work remotely across all of Russia. In Chita we can meet in person if needed.

Can AI be implemented in compliance with 152-FZ?

Yes. I use the Russian stack (YandexGPT, GigaChat) or local models on your own server — client data never leaves the country.

What does «turnkey» mean?

I take the task from audit and design all the way to a working solution and support. You get a finished result, not a set of instructions.

Can you help with 152-FZ and the notification to Roskomnadzor (RKN)?

Yes — an audit of data processing, preparation of documents, filing the notification to the register of operators and basic data protection. This is educational assistance, not legal advice.

How do we start working together?

Write to me on Telegram or by email and briefly describe the task. I'll suggest a format, timeline and an honest estimate.
